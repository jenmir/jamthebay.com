To submit jam session reviews, email the webmaster.  
When sending reviews, please include:

- Location (web site preferred)

 - Dates and Times

 - Name of host

 - Skill level (open, pro, invitation-only)

 - Genres permitted (blues, rock, jazz, anything-goes)

 - Anything else relevant or useful for jammers! e.g. parking, door cover etc. 

We also welcome updates on existing sessions, and any news about ones that are defunct or on hiatus.

Reviews may be edited for content and brevity before publishing. 

Your email will not be published in the review, but we will need your initials to identifiy reviewers to our readers.

Armando's, Martinez


Where: Armando's, 707 Marina Vista, Martinez   925.228.6985  

ANNOUNCEMENT JAN 2020
We are sad to announce that the owner of the building where Armando's is located has served a 30-day eviction notice. Accordingly, Armando's will be closed indefinitely after February 9th, 2020. We have had a great time over the last 14+ years, and loved every minute! So much talent has graced our stage, and we have had tremendous love and support from the artists, the patrons and our team. Roy Jeans will be looking for an alternative location, and we are hopeful Armando's will rise up again and continue to be a cherished part of the musical landscape in Martinez.

Vacaville (formerly Vallejo) Jazz Jam

When: first or second Saturdays (call ahead) 12-2pm

Where: Community Presbyterian Church, 425 Hemlock St, Vacaville, CA 95688

Host: Patricia Kutza 707.319.4184 or pkutza at pacbell.net

Formerly at the Methodist Church. This is a jazz session reading Real Books. Note from Host: All levels of musicians (including singers) welcome for non-competitive and supportive environment. You must bring instruments and amps if needed. Repertoire primarily based on Jazz standards fakebooks (and tunes/lead sheets provided in advance). Host requests a $5 donation, the full proceeds of which goes to support Church community outreach projects. (Updated Sep 2021 by PK)

Host has re-confirmed this session as of Sep 2021 at a new location. See Facebook session link for updates.
