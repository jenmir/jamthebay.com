To submit jam session reviews, email the webmaster.  
When sending reviews, please include:

- Location (web site preferred)

 - Dates and Times

 - Name of host

 - Skill level (open, pro, invitation-only)

 - Genres permitted (blues, rock, jazz, anything-goes)

 - Anything else relevant or useful for jammers! e.g. parking, door cover etc. 

We also welcome updates on existing sessions, and any news about ones that are defunct or on hiatus.

Reviews may be edited for content and brevity before publishing. 

Your email will not be published in the review, but we will need your initials to identifiy reviewers to our readers.

The Bistro, Hayward

When: First Sunday Monthly jazz session, 4pm
When: Second and Fourth Sundays blues sessions, 4pm
When: First Wednesday Monthly jazz session, 9pm,

Where: The Bistro, 1001 B Street, Hayward  510.886.8525

Hayward's best-known live music venue has multiple sessions. Send a review!

(From the Wednesday session host Poh Teng:)
The house band starts at 9pm. We take a break and open up the session. Anyone who wants to play should come talk to us during the break. Our usual crowd of players who walk in are Berkeley Jazz School, CSUEB students and alumni and a lot of pro players in the area. We have had guests come from out of town to sit in as well. (Updated Apr 2012)


Armando's, Martinez

When: 4th Mondays blues jam 7-10pm (host: Good Stuff Guitar Shop)
When: 3rd Mondays jazz jam 7-10pm  (host: All Of Us)

Where: Armando's, 707 Marina Vista, Martinez   925.228.6985  Calendar

Host: Good Stuff Guitars personnel (varies)   925-228-2500

We have heard good things about the blues jam at Armando's (signups at 6:30pm). The jazz session we know much less about.  Send a review!  There is a $5 door cover for this session.

Cover for all jams is $5.  Doors/sign-ups ~7, jams 7:30-10p.
Greg Reginato is no longer hosting the Jazz Jam.  The new host is 'All Of Us' which is essentially a house band put together by the guy leading.
Good Stuff Guitar Shop, owned by Danny White, sponsors the Blues Jam. Blues Jam lead rotates through several people.  House band is guitar/bass/drum/harp/kbd.
Marina Vista exit off I-680.  Right next to the Amtrak station.  Usually parking is easy, and free after 6.  (Updated Apr 2015 by JG)

 There are some YouTube videos from recent Monday nite Blues Jams at Armando's. (Updated Jul 2012 by TD)


Ivy Room, Albany

When: Tuesday evenings  9pm-12:30am

Where: Ivy Room, 860 San Pablo Ave, Albany  510.526.5888

Host: Mike Toth

New session as of Jan 2016, with Facebook Link here. No signups, no slots, fluid situations. Full backline including guitar amps. Song selections tend toward funk, classic soul and jazz of the non-reading and horn-friendly sort; expect to see horn players every session. As with all new sessions, recommend messaging host on FB to confirm the jam is still on. Send a review!


Marina Lounge, San Leandro

When: Thursday evenings 9pm

Where: Marina Lounge, 13880 Catalina St., SL   510.483.8622

Host: Varies

Jam is ongoing. Open to varied genres - blues, rock, soul. Signup board for instruments  before 9pm. No cover.


It could stand to have a little more structure as dominant personalities can monopolize the scene. There are different skill levels, with most performers having had substantial live performance experience. Friendly bunch, conversational and generally welcoming. It was funny last week with 5-6 bassists showing up. There was a substitute MC that was so-so. A frontman took the mic early and overshared. I noticed every week the volume levels are briefly suppressed so that a flutist can put in her contributions.  I heard about the session from a friend who sings and plays blues harp. It's a bit of local color. Location is industrial part of San Leandro that is safe, with easy parking. (Updated Aug 2014 by AC)

Chris' Club, Vallejo

When: Monday night blues jam 7-11pm

Where: Chris' Club, 656 Benicia Road, Vallejo  707-552-2916

Host: Bay Area Blues Society, Ronnie Stewart

This is a terrible jam. I've gone to this jam a few times and they have a list that's thrown away if any of the hosts friends show up, which is often. If you go a few times, you will notice there are few regulars in terms of the musicians who show up.  There's a reason for that.  If you're a glutton for rude hosts and favoritism, then by all means this jam is for you. (Updated May 2015 by JH)
