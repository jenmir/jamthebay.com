To submit jam session reviews, email the webmaster.  
When sending reviews, please include:

- Location (web site preferred)

 - Dates and Times

 - Name of host

 - Skill level (open, pro, invitation-only)

 - Genres permitted (blues, rock, jazz, anything-goes)

 - Anything else relevant or useful for jammers! e.g. parking, door cover etc. 

We also welcome updates on existing sessions, and any news about ones that are defunct or on hiatus.

Reviews may be edited for content and brevity before publishing. 

Your email will not be published in the review, but we will need your initials to identifiy reviewers to our readers.

23 Club, Brisbane

When: Wednesday pro blues session  8pm

Where: 23 Club, 23 Visitacion Ave, Brisbane  415.467.7717

Host: varies

23 Club has had sessions off and on over the years.

I stopped by the club in January. The jammers started rolling in around 8:30pm, mostly guitars and some percussion too. Some players bring an amp, some don't. The house band plays a mix of R&B, funk and blues, and promises "everyone gets to play." There are regular jammers that seem to know each other well. Bar is chilly inside, so dress in layers. Pizza takeout open next door.  (updated Jan 2014 by SJ)


Winters Tavern, Pacifica

Where: Winters Tavern, 1522 Francisco Blvd, Pacifica  650.355.6162

Host: DC Power & Electric Company  Session Site

Confirmed by host. Recent guests include Chris Cobb, Stan Erhart, Tia Carroll, Terry Hiatt. (Updated Mar 2014 by SJ)

Club Fox, Redwood City

When: Most Wednesday nights 7-11pm (check calendar to confirm)

Where: Fox Theater, 2223 Broadway St., RC  650.369.7770

Host: Vince Caminiti

Pro-level blues jam with high level of musicianship, and rotating excellent featured bands so you're not hearing the same house band every time. Guitarists galore, not too many horns. (Horn players will not get to play all night though.) The session has been off and on recently due to changes in ownership of Fox Theater. Lately it seems back on again, and we would appreciate a recent review from anyone brave enough to try it.  There is a $5 door cover for this session.

Sodinis, Redwood City

When: Wednesday nights blues session, 8-11pm

Where: Sodinis, 727 El Camino Real, RC  650.369.8000  

Host: Harris Fullbright & West Bay Blues Band

Very casual, local and informal blues-oriented session at the local dive bar. Occasionally rock and soul creeps into the songlist. When the house band is up expect to hear Fats Domino, Frogman, Coasters.  Open to all skill levels.  Horn players can pretty much play through the night. If you're not into the pressure-cooker pro-level scene at the Fox, give this session a try on a Wednesday. No cover, street parking on ECR or Brewster. (Updated Apr 2015 by SJ)


Hola Mexican Restaurant, Belmont

When: Monday nights open session 7-11pm

Where: Hola Mexican Restaurant and Cantina, 1015 Alameda de las Pulgas
Belmont  650. 591.1735

Host: Sweet Talk Band

This session bills itself as an "open mic" probably because they accept acoustic acts. BUT yet there is a full backline and amplification and youtube videos of people playing louder stuff. So there's that. Open to pretty much anything and everyone. Send a review!


Community School of Music & Arts, Mountain View

When: Jazz session (generally) last Sunday of the month, 1-5:30pm
Where: CSMA, 230 San Antonio Circle, Mountain View
Host: Marty Honda   650.823.8013

The music school in Mountain View is the site of a monthly open jazz session that has been going on for some years now. The format is a jazz clinic with advance sign-ups required. There is a FAQ from the host.   NOTE: There is a $10 fee to participate in this session if you are not a CSMA student.


Cheers, Pacifica

When: First Saturday evenings monthly

Where: Cheers, in Linda Mar across from Taco Bell 650.359.1225

Host: Chris Butterworth & Rafa Valenzuela

This divey club announced they are starting up a monthly Saturday Night jam session on the first Sat. of the month. Call the club to confirm ahead of time. I have not done the session but I have played there *many* times and the acoustics of the place are amazing - great place for live recording. I was there there the other night and discussed personally with club management, who confirmed the jam is on and horn players are most welcome. Please send a review!  (Updated Jan 2011 by SJ)
